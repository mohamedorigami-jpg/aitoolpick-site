What Is Airies?

Airies is an AI education platform built in Switzerland, designed to make artificial intelligence practical for everyday professional use. Rather than trying to cover every AI tool available, it focuses on a curated set of tools and structured learning paths organised into 8 progressive levels - from foundational concepts all the way through to AI for finance, trading, and business leadership.

The platform targets three main audiences: students starting from zero, professionals who want to automate their work and move faster, and teams or organisations looking for structured AI training across departments. It has 10,000+ learners and is used by students at some of the most respected universities in Europe, including Oxford, LSE, ETH Zürich, and Maastricht.

What You Actually Get

The curriculum is structured into 8 levels, each unlocking a more advanced set of skills. Level 1 covers the basics of how AI works and how to prompt effectively. By Level 4 you're designing AI agent workflows and building department-level automation. Levels 7 and 8 are aimed at entrepreneurs, business leaders, and people interested in AI for finance and trading.

Each level is practical, not theoretical. The platform avoids academic jargon and focuses on real workflows you can use at work the same week. You can follow the levels in order or jump to the one most relevant to your current role.

The platform also includes an AI tools directory, news and blog content, and an events calendar for AI conferences and workshops across Europe - making it more of an ongoing resource than a one-time course purchase.

Is Airies Right for UK Professionals?

If you are a professional who keeps saying "I need to get better at AI" but hasn't found a structured way in, Airies is a strong option. The free plan removes all risk - you can work through five real courses before spending anything, and the individual plan at around £22/month is cheaper than most competitors offering comparable depth.

It is not the right pick if you want a quick certification to put on a CV without actually learning anything. The curriculum is genuinely progressive and takes investment. For students, the university access is worth checking first - if your institution is on the list, you may already have free access.

One caveat for UK buyers: prices are in euros, so your monthly cost will shift slightly with exchange rates. Not a dealbreaker, but worth knowing.

Our Verdict
Airies is one of the better-structured AI learning platforms available in 2026. The free plan is genuinely useful, the curriculum is practical rather than academic, and the university partnerships give it credibility that most new EdTech platforms lack. At around £22/month for full access, it delivers solid value for professionals who want to build real AI skills - not just watch videos.
