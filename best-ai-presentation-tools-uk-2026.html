AI presentations built inside the Canva ecosystem you already know

Canva added AI presentation generation to its platform and it is a logical step for the millions of UK businesses already using Canva for social media graphics, logos, and marketing materials. Pitch deck specialist for startups and freelancers on a budget
3.8 / 5.0

Slidebean targets startups and freelancers who need a polished pitch deck without hiring a designer. The AI analyses your content and builds a presentation optimised for the pitch format. It also includes a library of investor-ready financial models and case studies.
