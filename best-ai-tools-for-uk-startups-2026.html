How to Choose

These four tools cover different parts of running a startup. The question is not which is best, it is which you need first.

If you are a solo founder just getting started, Notion AI is the obvious first move. It costs nothing, it is flexible, and it grows with you. Get your workspace set up in an afternoon and use the AI to draft your first business plan.

If content marketing is your main growth channel and you need to get your website, emails, and social copy done without a copywriter, Jasper earns its cost quickly. The trial is cheap enough to validate in a week.

If you are already juggling multiple tools and losing time to context switching, ClickUp consolidates your workflow and its free plan means there is no cost to try it.

If your problem is time - you are in meetings all day and have no hours left to actually build - Motion is the fix. It is worth the cost once you have paying customers.

Our Verdict
Notion AI is the obvious first tool for any UK startup founder. It is free, it covers the most bases, and the AI layer makes it genuinely useful from day one. Add Jasper once you need content, ClickUp if your team grows past two people, and Motion when your calendar becomes your biggest productivity problem. None of these require a big commitment. All of them replace something more expensive.
