What Is QuickBooks?

QuickBooks is an accounting platform designed for businesses that want to manage their finances efficiently. It handles invoicing, expenses, VAT, and payroll in one place. UK businesses use it to track cash flow, prepare tax returns, and stay on top of bookkeeping.

The platform is cloud-based, which means you do not need to install anything. You sign up, connect your accounts, and get going. It works with most browsers and has apps for both desktop and mobile.
