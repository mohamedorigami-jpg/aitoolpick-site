What Is Mindbody?

Mindbody is a comprehensive fitness business management platform founded in the US but now operating globally. It serves over 60,000 health and wellness businesses worldwide. The software combines class scheduling, member payments, staff management, and marketing automation in one system.

For UK small businesses, Mindbody offers British English support, VAT-compliant invoicing, and integration with UK payment processors. The platform also connects to ClassPass, which can bring new members through the door.

Key Features

Mindbody excels at automating the day-to-day running of a fitness business. The class scheduling system lets members book themselves online 24/7, reducing admin time significantly. Automated email and SMS reminders cut no-show rates, which can save a small studio hundreds of pounds per month in lost revenue.

The marketing suite includes pre-built campaigns for new member welcome sequences, re-engagement emails for inactive members, and birthday offers. These can be set up in minutes and run automatically. The reporting dashboard shows revenue trends, class popularity, and member retention metrics.

For businesses with multiple locations, Mindbody offers centralised management. Staff can be scheduled across sites, and owners get a unified view of performance.

Is Mindbody Right for UK Small Businesses?

Mindbody makes the most sense for UK gyms and studios with at least 50 active members. Below that number, the monthly cost is hard to justify against simpler, cheaper alternatives like Calendly or even Excel-based tracking.

If you run a small PT studio, yoga shala, or boutique fitness space with under 30 members, look at cheaper options first. But if you are scaling toward 100+ members and spending hours each week on admin, Mindbody pays for itself through saved time and reduced no-shows.

Our Verdict
Mindbody is a solid choice for growing UK fitness businesses with 50+ members. The price is higher than basic tools, but the automation and marketing features save real time. Small studios should start with the Basic plan and upgrade only when needed.
