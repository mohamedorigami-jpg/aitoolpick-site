About AI Tool Pick

Last updated: 28 April 2026

What we do

AI Tool Pick is an independent review site that helps UK small businesses find the right AI tools. We test software, compare features, and publish honest reviews - no fluff, no paid placements.

Every review includes real GBP pricing, VAT considerations, and UK-specific context you will not find on US-focused sites. We answer one question: should a UK small business actually spend money on this tool?

Who runs this

The Team

AI Tool Pick is run by a small team of UK-based researchers, writers and developers who have spent years working with digital tools, e-commerce, and SaaS. Every tool is tested against real UK business scenarios before we publish a review.

We keep our reviews independent - no paid placements, no sponsored content. Every article you see comes from hands-on testing and honest assessment.

Why we started

Most AI tool review sites are US-focused and push whatever pays the highest affiliate commission. We wanted something different: a UK-focused resource that actually tests tools against real business needs.

In 2026, UK small businesses are bombarded with AI tools priced in dollars, with no VAT transparency and support that only covers US hours. AI Tool Pick exists to cut through that noise.

How we review

  • We sign up for paid accounts and test tools for at least 7 days
  • We verify GBP pricing including VAT where applicable
  • We check UK support hours, data residency, and GDPR compliance
  • We never accept payment for reviews or placement
  • We update reviews quarterly or when major features change

How we make money

We earn commissions through affiliate links when you buy a tool through our site. This costs you nothing extra. We only recommend tools we have actually tested or researched thoroughly. Read our full affiliate disclosure.

Our principles

  • Independent - no paid reviews, no sponsored placements
  • UK-focused - pricing in GBP, relevant to UK regulations and business context
  • Honest - we point out flaws, not just benefits
  • Practical - every review answers "should a small business actually buy this?"
