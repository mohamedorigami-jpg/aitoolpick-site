What Is Fathom AI?

Fathom is an AI meeting assistant that sits inside your video calling platform and handles the note-taking for you. It records the call, transcribes everything in real time, and generates an AI summary with key points and action items as soon as the meeting ends. You can replay specific moments, create highlight clips, and search across all your past meetings.

Fathom was founded in the US and has grown primarily through the sales and revenue operations market. In 2024 it raised a Series A and has expanded into team collaboration features. It competes directly with Otter.ai and tl;dv. For UK small businesses, the main appeal is simple: stop taking notes and start paying attention in meetings.

Key Features

Fathom works as a Chrome extension and desktop app. You install it, connect your calendar, and it automatically joins and records any Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams meeting you schedule. The bot does not appear in the meeting as a visible participant - it records audio directly from your device.

Transcription is fast and accurate for clear English speech. The AI summary generates within seconds of a call ending, breaking down what was discussed, decisions made, and any next steps. Premium users get access to over 15 expert summary templates including BANT, Sandler, and MEDDIC - useful if your team runs structured sales or discovery calls.

The AI action items feature automatically assigns tasks from what was said in the meeting. The follow-up email draft is generated from the meeting content and can be sent directly from the app. You can also ask Fathom questions about your meeting in a conversational chat interface, similar to querying a document.

For teams, the Business plan includes CRM sync with Salesforce, HubSpot, and other platforms, automatically pushing meeting summaries into your CRM records. Coaching metrics track talking ratios and other behavioural patterns in calls, which is most useful for sales team managers.

Is Fathom AI Right for UK Small Businesses?

If your business runs more than a handful of video calls each week, Fathom is worth installing for free and seeing how you get on. The free plan covers everything you need for solo use: unlimited recordings, transcriptions, and a decent AI summary. That alone is more generous than Otter.ai's free tier, which caps meeting minutes.

Small sales teams and client-facing consultants will get the most value from Premium or Team plans. The AI follow-up email draft alone saves real time after every client call. The template-based summaries (BANT, Sandler, MEDDIC) are useful for anyone running structured sales processes. Teams who need CRM integration and coaching analytics should budget for Business, though at ~£20/month per user the cost adds up quickly for larger teams.

Businesses that handle sensitive client data should verify Fathom's current GDPR documentation directly, as the company's US base means standard data processing terms may require additional review.

Our Verdict
Fathom AI earns its recommendation on the strength of its free plan alone. For solo UK small business owners running daily client calls, it removes the friction of note-taking without adding a subscription cost. Upgrade to Premium when you need unlimited AI summaries and follow-up email drafts. Team and Business plans are better suited to sales operations teams where CRM sync and coaching metrics justify the per-user cost.
