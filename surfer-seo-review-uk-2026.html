What Is Surfer SEO?

Surfer SEO is a seo platform designed for businesses that want to save time on repetitive seo tasks. It uses AI to handle the work so your team does not have to do it manually. UK businesses use it for everything from content creation to workflow automation.

The platform is cloud-based, which means you do not need to install anything. You sign up, connect your accounts, and get going. It works with most browsers and has apps for both desktop and mobile.
